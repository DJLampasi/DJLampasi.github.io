about

It always starts with a dream...

Inspired by the likes of Steve Aoki, Blasterjaxx and Dannic, DJ Jayden Lampasi started mixing the beats at fourteen. With only a few resources to start with, he has developed his skills to transform his dream into a reality.

Jayden has utilised his passion for music to transform popular songs into unique remixes. Starting off as a weekend hobby, Jayden has progressed to DJ'ing across Western Sydney at house parties, birthday celebrations and even under 18s music festivals where he cranks the volume and blasts the music, getting the party pumping!

Jayden is a versatile musician as he experiments with Electro House, Melbourne Bounce, R&B and the very latest of pop hits. Working hard creating music which people enjoy, it must be paying off!

Jayden works his way up the chart and envisions that one day he may be able to DJ internationally and that his music may be enjoyed by many more party enthusiasts.
